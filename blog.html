Mt. Naupa

Mt. Naupa, is a scenic mountain peak located in Naga City, Cebu. Here's something exciting about it: Sea of Clouds Experience. One of the most thrilling experiences at Mt. Naupa is witnessing the sea of clouds. This natural phenomenon occurs when fog blankets the landscape, creating a breathtaking view that looks like the mountains are floating on a sea of clouds2. It's a magical sight that's best experienced early in the morning or after a rainy day.
